Biography

I am sitting for 2 hours trying to write something special about myself, but nothing interesting gets into my head – just obvious things and some of-color jokes… Well, there were some local victories in my life, but I think, failures happened more often. From a certain time, I started looking at myself with a touch of irony and take setbacks as statistical norm. However, is it a best LUCK to be born as a human and to live life having opportunity of informed choice?
